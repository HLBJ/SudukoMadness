Privacy Policy
Last updated: 11 Jan. 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sudoku Master (“the Game”) handles user data.

1. Information We Collect
    The Game does not collect personally identifiable information directly.
    However, third-party services integrated into the Game may collect limited, anonymous data such as:
        •   Device type
        •   Operating system
        •   Advertising identifiers
        •   Gameplay interactions (for ad performance)
    This data is used solely to:
        •   Display advertisements
        •   Measure ad performance
        •   Improve stability and user experience

2. Third-Party Services
The Game uses the following third-party services:
        •   Unity LevelPlay (IronSource)
        •   MixKid.co (Sound Effects)
        •   kenney.nl (Icons)
These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.
We encourage you to review their policies:
Unity Ads / LevelPlay Privacy Policy

3. Advertisements
    The Game displays:
        •   Banner ads
        •   Interstitial ads
        •   Rewarded video ads
    Rewarded ads are optional and provide in-game benefits such as hints.

4. Children’s Privacy
    The Game does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.
    If you believe that personal data has been collected from a child, please contact us so we can remove it.

5. Data Storage
    •   Game settings and progress may be stored locally on your device.
    •   Cloud features (Later Update) are used only for syncing progress and settings.

6. Changes to This Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
    Changes will take effect immediately after being published in the Game.
